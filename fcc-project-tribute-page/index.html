Manu Ginobili

The biggest robbery in NBA history


Here's a time line of Manu Ginobili's career:

  • 1977 Born in Bahia Blanca, Argentina.
  • 1995 Starts his professional basketball career playing for Andino Sport Club of La Rioja, Argentina.
  • 1999 San Antonio Spurs selected him in the 2nd Round / 57th Pick as Shooting Guard. The reason why Manu is (and will be) the biggest robbery in NBA history is because no one imagined that such a good player would be selected in such a distant instance.
  • 2002 Joins the Spurs and soon became a key player for the team. Along with Tony Parker and Tim Duncan as teammates, they were known as the "Big Three" of the Spurs for almost fifteen years.
  • 2003 Wins his first NBA Finals title against New Jersey Nets.
  • 2004 Representing Argentina, wins his first and unique Gold medal against Italy at Summer Olympics in Athenas.
  • 2005 Wins his second NBA Finals title against Detroit Pistons.
  • 2007 Wins his third NBA Finals title against Cleveland Cavaliers.
  • 2014 Wins his fourth NBA Finals title against Miami Heat.
  • 2018 Announces his retirement from professional basketball.
  • 2019 Spurs retires Ginobili's No. 20 jersey.
